Bangtan Boys

BTS (방탄소년단) consists of 7 members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V and Jungkook. BTS debuted June 13, 2013, under Big Hit Entertainment, with the title track ‘No More Dream’ from the album ‘2 Cool 4 Skool’.
